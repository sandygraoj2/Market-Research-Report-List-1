<p><p>The discrete diodes market encompasses various types, each serving unique applications. Schottky diodes are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching speeds, making them ideal for power applications. Transient Suppression Diodes (TVS) protect circuits from voltage spikes, ensuring component safety. Commutation diodes manage current in inductive loads, improving efficiency in switching devices. Other diodes include Zener and rectifier diodes, catering to specialized needs in electronics, thus driving innovation and growth in the market.</p></p>

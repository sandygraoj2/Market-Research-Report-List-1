<p><p>Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) software is categorized into cloud-based and on-premises types. Cloud-based RPM uses internet-hosted services, allowing easy access to patient data from any location. It facilitates real-time monitoring, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as it reduces the need for extensive on-site infrastructure. On-premises RPM, on the other hand, involves installing software on local servers within healthcare facilities. This type offers enhanced control over data security and compliance but may require significant investment in hardware and maintenance.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Steam Dried(SD)</li><li>Flame Dried(FD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fish meal feed market primarily consists of two types: Steam Dried (SD) and Flame Dried (FD). SD fish meal is produced using steam heat, preserving nutrients and enhancing digestibility, making it ideal for aquaculture and livestock feed. In contrast, FD fish meal employs direct flame for drying, resulting in a higher protein content but potentially lower digestibility. Both types cater to specific nutritional needs in animal feed, influencing demand based on quality, price, and application in various farming sectors.</p></p>

<p><p>The wood fibre board market consists primarily of two types: porous wood fibre boards and hard wood fibre boards. Porous wood fibre boards are lightweight and offer excellent insulation and sound absorption properties, making them ideal for construction and interior applications. In contrast, hard wood fibre boards are denser and more durable, providing superior strength and resistance to wear, often used in furniture and flooring. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, driving innovation and sustainability in building materials.</p></p>

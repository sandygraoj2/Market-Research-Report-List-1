<p><strong>The Antiblocking Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organic</li><li>Inorganic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antiblocking agents market consists of two primary types: organic and inorganic. Organic antiblocking agents, derived from natural materials such as waxes and fatty acids, enhance the surface quality of plastic films by preventing sticking. In contrast, inorganic antiblocking agents, typically composed of silica or calcium carbonate, provide a more durable solution, improving the mechanical properties and transparency of plastics. Both types are essential in various industries, including packaging, to ensure smooth production and functionality of plastic products.</p></p>

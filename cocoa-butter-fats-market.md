<p><p>The cocoa butter fats market comprises three main types: Cocoa Butter Equivalents (CBE), Cocoa Butter Substitutes (CBS), and others. CBE closely resembles cocoa butter in physical and chemical properties, enabling manufacturers to replace cocoa butter in formulations while maintaining quality. CBS, on the other hand, is derived from various oils and designed to mimic cocoa butter's characteristics but may differ in taste and melting behavior. Other products in this market include various blends and novel formulations catering to specific applications.</p></p>

<p><p>A clamp-on flow meter is a non-invasive measuring device used to determine the flow rate of liquids and gases in pipes. It operates by attaching ultrasonic transducers on the exterior of the pipe, allowing for accurate flow measurement without interrupting the system or requiring physical modifications.

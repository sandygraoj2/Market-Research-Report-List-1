<p><p>The discrete and chip antenna market is divided into two primary types: dual band and single band antennas. In contrast, dual band antennas support two frequency bands, providing versatility for applications that require multiple connectivity options, such as LTE and Wi-Fi. These distinctions influence their use in various devices, from smartphones to IoT applications, driving demand for tailored solutions in the wireless communication sector.</p></p>

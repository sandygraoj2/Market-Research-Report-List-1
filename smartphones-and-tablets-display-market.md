<p><p>The smartphone and tablet display market consists of several key types. IPS (In-Plane Switching) displays offer wide viewing angles and accurate color reproduction, making them popular for high-quality visuals. TFT (Thin-Film Transistor) displays are cost-effective and commonly used in budget devices due to their good performance, though they have limited viewing angles. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays provide vibrant colors, deep blacks, and energy efficiency, enhancing user experience. Other display technologies include AMOLED and mini-LED, catering to varying consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>

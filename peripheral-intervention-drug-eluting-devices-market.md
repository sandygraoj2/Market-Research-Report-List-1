<p><strong>The Peripheral Intervention Drug-Eluting Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drug-Eluting Balloons</li><li>Drug-Eluting Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peripheral Intervention Drug-Eluting Devices market primarily comprises two main types: Drug-Eluting Balloons (DEBs) and Drug-Eluting Stents (DESs). Drug-Eluting Balloons are coated with medication that is delivered directly into the arterial wall during balloon angioplasty, helping to prevent restenosis. Drug-Eluting Stents are mesh-like tubes embedded with drugs that release medication over time to inhibit tissue growth after vascular procedures. Both technologies aim to improve patient outcomes by reducing the risk of re-narrowing in treated blood vessels.</p></p>

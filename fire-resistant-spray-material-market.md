<p><strong>The Fire-resistant Spray Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire-resistant spray material market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based sprays use water as a primary solvent, offering low volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and a reduced environmental impact. They are easier to clean and have a faster drying time. Solvent-based sprays, on the other hand, utilize organic solvents for application, providing superior adhesion and durability in harsher conditions. Both types are essential in various industries for enhancing fire safety and meeting regulatory standards.</p></p>

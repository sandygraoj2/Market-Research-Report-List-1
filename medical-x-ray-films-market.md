<p><p>The medical X-ray films market can be categorized into three main types: Green, Half Speed Blue, and Full Speed Blue. Green X-ray films are sensitive to green light and offer excellent detail with reduced radiation doses. Half Speed Blue films require moderate radiation exposure and provide a balance between image quality and exposure. Full Speed Blue films are highly sensitive to blue light, allowing for the shortest exposure times, making them ideal for rapid imaging and minimizing patient exposure.</p></p>

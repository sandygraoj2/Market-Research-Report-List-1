<p><p>Tungsten CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing) slurries for metal removal are categorized into two main types based on their selection ratios: high selection ratio and low selection ratio. High selection ratio slurries are designed for selective removal of tungsten, enabling precise patterning with minimal damage to surrounding materials, making them ideal for advanced semiconductor applications. In contrast, low selection ratio slurries provide a more uniform removal rate, suitable for less demanding processes where broader material removal is required.</p></p>
